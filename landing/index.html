Features Of Expense Controller

Expense Tracking

Log all expenses with details, categorize by type, and view transaction history to better understand spending habits.

Budget Management

Set monthly or custom budgets, track spending against your limits, and receive alerts when approaching budget thresholds.

Financial Reports

Visualize spending patterns with detailed charts and graphs, including monthly summaries and category breakdowns for better financial insights.

Group Expense Sharing:

Easily share expenses with friends or family, split bills, and keep track of shared spending in groups for clear accountability.
